A nonpartisan civic platform that brings voter guidance, civic education, candidate research, and participation tools together in one place.

Our Mission

Civics Lantern exists to make civic participation easier to understand and access. Reliable civic information is often scattered across dozens of websites, buried in confusing language. Our mission is to bring civic education, state-by-state voting guidance, research, and action resources into one clear, always nonpartisan place.

“An informed citizenry is at the heart of a dynamic democracy.” — Thomas Jefferson

Understand

Clear, digestible explanations of elections, offices, and civic systems.

Practical voting guidance, deadlines, ID rules, and official state resources.

Explore

Act
