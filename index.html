Our Mission

Civics Lantern exists to make civic participation easier to understand and access. Reliable civic information is often scattered across dozens of websites, buried in confusing language. Our mission is to bring civic education, state-by-state voting guidance, research, and action resources into one clear, always nonpartisan place.
